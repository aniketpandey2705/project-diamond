How Blockchain Verification Works

  • Cryptographic Hashing: Each block contains a SHA-256 hash of the previous block
  • Chain Integrity: Any tampering breaks the hash chain and is immediately detected
  • Immutability: Once recorded, grievance data cannot be altered or deleted
  • Transparency: All blocks can be independently verified by anyone
